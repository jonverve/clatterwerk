1 · Three boards in separate places, one machine

Three boards mounted in different DOM locations, joined into a group. Each button fires a single CustomEvent; the group listens and all boards answer together — values can also arrive from a WebSocket, a poll, or any other trigger source the same way.

2 · A single board & the three modes

Cascade: all cells clatter at once at constant speed and stop independently — the live-update mode. Flywheel: spin-up, blur, hard stop — the page-load showpiece. Single: one quiet flip per cell.

3 · 3D flip off (classic flat look)

Same engine, threeD: false — no shared perspective, tilt, pop, overhang, shading, or slam theatrics. Quieter, fully contained in each cell.
